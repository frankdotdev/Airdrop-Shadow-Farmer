# Market Positioning

Shadow Farmer targets Web3 users seeking automated airdrop farming without risks.

## Target Audience

- **Crypto Enthusiasts**: Early adopters farming airdrops.
- **DeFi Users**: Multi-chain traders needing yield.
- **NFT Collectors**: Farming NFT airdrops.
- **Developers**: Building on Web3, testing strategies.

## Market Size

- Web3 users: 100M+.
- Airdrop farmers: 10M active.
- TAM: $1B+ in airdrop economy.

## Competitors

- **Manual Tools**: TweetDeck, custom scripts (risky).
- **Other Bots**: Sybil Hunter, Airdrop King (less stealthy).
- **Exchanges**: Binance airdrops (not farming).
- **Unique Selling Points**: Non-custodial, human-mimicry, multi-chain.

## Value Proposition

- **Safe**: Non-custodial, encrypted.
- **Effective**: Human-like behavior avoids bans.
- **Comprehensive**: EVM + Solana, scanning, tracking.
- **Scalable**: Subscription model.

## Pricing Strategy

- Freemium: Basic tier free.
- Tiered: $10-50/month based on usage.
- Referrals: 10% discount for invites.

## Go-to-Market

- **Telegram**: Mini App distribution.
- **Twitter/Discord**: Community building.
- **Partnerships**: With protocols for exclusive airdrops.
- **Marketing**: Content on airdrop strategies, case studies.

## Monetization

- Subscriptions: 80% revenue.
- Premium features: Custom strategies ($100/setup).
- Affiliates: 10% on referrals.

## Risks

- Regulatory: Airdrop farming may be seen as manipulation.
- Competition: New bots emerge.
- Bans: Protocols detect automation.

## Growth Plan

- Q1: MVP launch, 1000 users.
- Q2: Add Solana, 10k users.
- Q3: Partnerships, 50k users.
- Q4: Mobile app, 100k users.

## Metrics

- MAU, retention, LTV, CAC.
- Farming success rate, user profits.

Position as the "safe, smart airdrop farmer" in Web3.
