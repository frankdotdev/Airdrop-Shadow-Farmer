# Pricing Guide

This guide covers pricing models for Shadow Farmer.

## Subscription Tiers

- **Hobbyist**: $0/month, 1 wallet, 10 farms, basic support.
- **Whales**: $10/month, unlimited wallets, advanced strategies, priority.
- **Protocol**: $50/month, custom farming, white-label, dedicated support.

## Pricing Strategy

- Freemium: Attract users, upsell.
- Value-based: Features justify price.
- Competitive: Cheaper than manual tools.

## Revenue Model

- Subscriptions: 80% revenue.
- One-time: Custom dev ($100+).
- Affiliates: 10% on referrals.

## Metrics

- LTV: $120/year avg.
- CAC: $5 via Telegram.
- Churn: 20%/month.

## Adjustments

- Discounts: Annual 20% off.
- Trials: 7 days free.
- Enterprise: Custom quotes.

Monitor and optimize.
